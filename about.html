About me

Welcome to my academic journey! It all began at Bharathiya Vidya Bhavan in Thrissur, a place where the seeds of my education were planted. Moving forward, I pursued a Bachelor of Science degree in Electronics at Paremkkavu College, delving deep into the intricacies of the subject and discovering my passion for electronics. The next chapter of my academic expedition unfolded at Nehru College in Pampady, where I undertook post-graduation studies. Nestled in the serene surroundings of Pampady, Nehru College provided advanced coursework and research opportunities that significantly contributed to shaping my expertise. With a diverse educational background spanning various institutions, I have cultivated a unique skill set. My journey is not just about acquiring knowledge but embracing a commitment to lifelong learning. Electronics is not merely a subject for me; it's a profound interest that has been the driving force behind my academic pursuits. Join me as I navigate the exciting landscape of knowledge and discovery, staying committed to continuous learning and exploring the fascinating realms of electronics and beyond
